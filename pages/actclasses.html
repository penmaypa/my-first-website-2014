  Hip hop Dance Classes


One of the oldest and the most important culture in Hip hop is dancing. People dance to express themselves. Here at Hip hop Culture Club, we like to express ourselves and our culture through dancing. Dancing has a lot of benefits. It is scientifically proven that dancing makes you fit, reduces stress, boost your memory, diminish depression and increase energy. The specific dance we practise here is hip-hop dance. Hip hop dance into divided into styles which is breaking, popping, locking and krumping.

We believe that any one can dance. If you can walk, you can dance. We have the best dance staff in Ireland. For the those who wants to learn to dance in any styles, we would be happy to teach you. We also have dance crew who performs from shows to concert. If you're fit enough to make dancing as your second nature, then we would be happy to let you join in our dance crew.
