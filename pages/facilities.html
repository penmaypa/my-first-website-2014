  FACILITIES


At Hip Hop Culture Club, we have the best facilities in town. Our facilities to facilitate social activities and classes. We have Dance Studio, Music & Recording Studio, Mini-Theatre and Game Room. Members of the Hip Hop Culture Club will have a full access to all facilities.

  Music & Recording Studio

We have a full professional Music & Recording Studio. Members of the club will have full access to the studio. The studio will also be used for educational purposes, for people who wants to learn to use the studio equipment. Members can record and produce their songs here.

  Dance Studio

Our Dance Studio is available for all dancers. This is where the dancing happens. The dance studio is big enough to facilitate 20 - 30 people. In the dance studio, we provide good quality sound surround system for better sound and stronger bass. Mirrors will also be provided within the dance classes. We also have home gym equipment for fitness purposes.

  Game Room

We like to play and have fun. The Game Room is the place to be when it come for fun. In the Game Room , we have the every game consoles including PS-1, PS-2, Ps-3, Xbox360, XboxOne, Nintendo Wii, Arcade Video Game and many more. We also have pool table,table tennis, dance platform and dart boards. Members of the club can hangout and socialize in the game room. They have full access to every game consoles.
