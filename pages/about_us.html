  About the Hip hop Culture Club




Hip hop Culture Club is a club for all the people who shares the same interest in Hip hop culture. The members of this club mostly have a background in rapping, break dancing or producing. Here they can hangout, play games, show off their rap skills and dance moves or they could justchill and relax anytime with other people. This is a great place to meet people who shares the same interest and passion in Hip hop. Here in the Hip hop Culture Club, we provide activities and classes to the members. The activities include rap battle, beats making and Dance Showdown. Members can also learn to dj and learn new dance steps in the dance class. The members of this club are from 18 years of age to above. All the members gather here every Tuesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday. Our club is located in Aungier street.
